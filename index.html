Welcome to CalmDown

CalmDown guides you through 4x4 breathing, a powerful technique to quickly reduce stress and anxiety.

  • Breathe in for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds

This simple practice can help calm your mind and body in just minutes. It's used by Navy SEALs, first responders, and mindfulness experts worldwide.

Regular practice may improve focus, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

For more information on the science behind 4x4 breathing, visit the Cleveland Clinic.
