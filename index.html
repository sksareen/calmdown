Welcome to CalmDown

Discover tranquility with our guided breathing exercises. Engage in 4x4 breathing, a technique proven to calm the mind and body within seconds.

This method involves inhaling for four counts, holding for four, exhaling for four, and pausing for four. It helps regulate your autonomic nervous system, reducing stress and enhancing focus.

Experience the benefits of 4x4 breathing: improved mental clarity, reduced anxiety, and a sense of relaxation. For more insights on this powerful technique, check out this top article.
