CalmDown Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15 2024

Information Collection and Use:

CalmDown uses eye-tracking technology to monitor your focus during exercises. No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted. All data related to your sessions is processed locally on your device.

Data Storage:

Any data generated during your use of CalmDown (such as exercise progress) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Data Security:

As all data is stored locally, the security of this data is managed by your browser's built-in security measures. We do not have access to this data and do not implement any additional security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
